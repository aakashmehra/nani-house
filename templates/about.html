ABOUT NANI HOUSE

Inspiration

Nani House was inspired by classic board games and modern strategy games, combining the tactical depth of chess with the unpredictability of dice-based adventures. The game draws inspiration from:

  • Traditional board games and their strategic gameplay mechanics
  • Modern multiplayer battle games with character abilities and progression
  • Roguelike elements that add unpredictability and replayability
  • Social gaming experiences that bring friends together

Our goal was to create a game that's easy to learn but challenging to master, where every match tells a unique story of strategy, luck, and epic battles.
