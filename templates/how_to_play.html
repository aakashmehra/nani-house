HOW TO PLAY

Game Overview

Nani House is a chaotic adventure combat board game on a 10×10 grid. Battle across the board, rolling dice, fighting enemies, and using abilities to be the last player standing or reach tile 100!

Players & Goal

• 2-6 players
• Reach tile 100, or
• Be the last player alive

Actions Per Turn

Roll: Roll a 6-sided dice and move forward
Attack: Attack enemies within range
Ability: Use your character's unique ability
Items: Use items from your inventory

Characters

Each character has unique stats (Health, Attack, Shield) and a special ability. Choose wisely!
